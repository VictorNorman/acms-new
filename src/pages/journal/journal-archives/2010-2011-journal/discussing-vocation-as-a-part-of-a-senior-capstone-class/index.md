**Maria Zack**, Point Loma Nazarene University (2010).

**Abstract**  
Many of us in Christian higher education are seeking ways to help our students discern their own sense of call. At the 2007 ACMS meeting Greg Crow and I participated in a panel on vocation and published a related paper in the Journal of the ACMS. This current paper discusses what has been learned from incorporating some of our ideas into our department’s senior capstone course. This has been very meaningful for both the students and the faculty involved.
