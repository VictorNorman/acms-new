**Johann deKlerk** (1999)

**Abstract**  
This paper reviews various techniques that have been used to teach mathematics from a Christian perspective. It argues that some of these methods are insufficient and that more attention needs to be paid to the historical, scientific, and religious context in which a mathematical subject is embedded.
