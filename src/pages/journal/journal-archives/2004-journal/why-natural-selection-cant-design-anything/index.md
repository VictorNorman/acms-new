**William Dembski** (2001).

**Abstract**  
This paper argues that known scientific laws cannot account for the key feature of life that needs to be explained – specified complexity. It carefully defines specified complexity and argues that the Darwinian mechanism operating via evolutionary algorithms is unable to explain it.
