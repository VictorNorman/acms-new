### Statement of Belief

We are an affiliation of Christians who trust in the good news of God’s saving work accomplished through Jesus Christ, as faithfully proclaimed by the Old and New Testaments, and as affirmed by the Nicene and Apostles’ creeds.

Those who wish to join our organization may read more at [Join the ACMS](http://www.acmsonline.org/membership/).

---

### Purposes of the ACMS

The ACMS has four purposes for impacting the lives of Christians in the mathematical sciences, each arising from and connected to our mission statement and statement of belief:

1.  To encourage exploration of the relationships among faith, scholarship, and teaching.
2.  To promote meaningful Christian interaction and relationships, providing a community in which there is no separation between faith life and professional life.
3.  To encourage research and writing, especially on topics exploring the relationships among faith, scholarship, and teaching.
4.  To promote innovative and effective teaching.
