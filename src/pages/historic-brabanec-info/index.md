# Bio

[Derek C. Schuurman](https://sites.calvin.edu/derek/) completed his undergrad and masters degrees at the University of Waterloo and worked in industry for several years designing embedded systems. Sensing a call to teaching, he completed a Ph.D. at McMaster University in the area of robotics and computer vision. He has taught computer science at Redeemer University, served as a visiting professor at Dordt University, and is currently professor of [computer science at Calvin University](https://computing.calvin.edu/ "https://computing.calvin.edu/") in Grand Rapids, Michigan. In 2003, when he began teaching, he attended his first ACMS conference hosted at Point Loma University and has been active in the ACMS ever since. He has served on the ACMS board (2011-2019) and on two ACMS conference organizing committees (2015 and 2022). He has written and spoken widely on technology and is a regular columnist with _[Christian Courier](https://www.christiancourier.ca/author/derek-schuurman1/ "https://www.christiancourier.ca/author/derek-schuurman1/")_ and contributor to the _[Christian Scholars Review blog](https://christianscholars.com/author/derek-c-schuurman/ "https://christianscholars.com/author/derek-c-schuurman/")_. He is author of the book _[Shaping a Digital World: Faith, Culture and Computer Technology](https://www.ivpress.com/shaping-a-digital-world "https://www.ivpress.com/shaping-a-digital-world")_ and co-author of the newly-released book, _[A Christian Field Guide to Technology for Engineers and Designers](https://www.ivpress.com/a-christian-field-guide-to-technology-for-engineers-and-designers "https://www.ivpress.com/a-christian-field-guide-to-technology-for-engineers-and-designers")_, both published by InterVarsity Academic Press. He and his wife, Carina, have four children, and he lives in Grand Rapids, Michigan.
