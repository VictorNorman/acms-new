## **Registration for ACMS Conference, 2024, at Dordt University is closed.**

The next ACMS biennial conference was held at Dordt University, Sioux Center, Iowa, from Wednesday, May 29 – Saturday, June 1, 2024.

Confirmed plenary speakers include:

*   ###### [Lydia Manikonda, RPI](https://faculty.rpi.edu/lydia-manikonda)
    
*   ###### [Joel Amidon, Ole Miss](https://olemiss.edu/people/jcamidon?id=jcamidon)
    
*   ###### [Judith Canner, CSU Monterey Bay](https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/faculty-staff/wang-award/past-years/2021-awardees/Pages/Judith-Canner.aspx)
    
*   ###### Jason Douma, University of Sioux Falls
    

Dordt is situated on a beautiful campus including a prairie, recreation center, and large water park across the street. Up-to-date accommodations include both apartments and dormitories.

Dordt will provide shuttles from the Sioux Falls airport (70 minutes) for a small fee. Sioux City has a limited number of flights (55 minutes) and Omaha will occasionally have prices that are worth the drive (2 hours 15 minutes).

(posted: 10/7/2023)
