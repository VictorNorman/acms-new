### On-Campus Accommodations

Air conditioned four bedroom townhouse style accommodations with two shared bathrooms, a common sitting area and a kitchenette. If you have mobility issues, please request to be placed in a ground level bedroom. Ground level bedrooms are limited and will be on a request basis only, until units are filled.

Each room has two single beds. Linens, one pillow, one blanket and daily towel service and complimentary hotel sized soap will be provided. Please note that blow driers, irons and toiletries such as shampoo and conditioner are not provided. Cooking is not permitted in the accommodations. WiFi is avialable for all guests.

Rates per night:

Single

CAD $50.00 + 13% HST tax

Double room with guest

CAD $60.00 + 13% HST tax

Double shared

CAD $30.00 + 13% HST tax

Because of the challenges with conversion rates, you will be directed to call Redeemer University College campus services to complete the reservations for your lodging, meals, and excursion. Instructions appear on the Check Out page of the online registration form.

A cancellation fee of $50.00 will be applied to all cancellations. To cancel your registration, please send request in writing to [Darlene Komadan](mailto:dkomadan@redeemer.ca) no later than May 16 to receive a refund. Any requests after May 16 will be on a consideration basis to quality for a partial refund.
